Rajan Chaubey

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Reviewed in India on 10 August 2020
This phone has its pros and cons.

PROS

1. Super responsive device (no lags at all)

2. Typical impressive Oxygen Os (i can nitpick few things though)

3. Good all round camera(front one could have ben better)

4. Superb gaming experience, this device is a beast for gaming.

5. Has USB-PD support too, didn't know till I used a pd charger in this device.

CONS

1. Black crush issue is there when set to 120Hz refresh rate, maybe can get fixed through an OTA and its not a deal breaker for me

2. Horizontal black bar is present from front camera area when set to 120Hz, don't know if it can be fixed with an OTA update or not, again not a deal breaker for me

Overall i am happy with my purchase. All I wanted a super fast and responsive device. I can ignore the display issues becz it appears in certain circumstances only, rest of the phone experience is amazing.
