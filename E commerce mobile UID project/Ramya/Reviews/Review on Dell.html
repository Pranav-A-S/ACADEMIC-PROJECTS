⭐⭐⭐

Reviewed in India on 5 March 2021
Pros:

Top notch configuration for student/ basic gaming and basic mutimedia purpose Sleek and nice design; Quite light for a 15.6 laptop Backlit keyboard and no complains about neither keyboard nor touchpad sensitivity Commendable display, and anti glare


Cons:

Heating is a concern but the fan does not make much noise, nor the system shuts due to overheat Battery life is only 4 hours running MS Word at 50% brightness (system update, scan not running) Screen frame width is pretty broad especially on the bottom (1.5 inches approx)


Overall:

Expensive against the configuration provided compared to other brands. But if you're a DELL fan, and looking for a latest 15.6 laptop, then this is a good call in terms of design and performance You can turn off the dynamic adaptive brightness (CABC feature) in BIOS settings
